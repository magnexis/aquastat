# Scientific Method

AquaStat distinguishes six evidence classes plus unknown:

- `Level A`: metered and verified
- `Level B`: officially reported
- `Level C`: corroborated observational
- `Level D`: derived
- `Level E`: modeled estimate
- `Level F`: unverified claim
- `Level U`: unknown

The platform prefers stronger evidence over weaker evidence and never silently converts a permit maximum, projection, or corroborated report into a metered actual.
